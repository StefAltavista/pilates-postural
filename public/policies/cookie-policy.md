## Necessary storage

This website uses browser local storage to remember the visitor's consent decision. It stores a small object containing:

- necessary: always true
- googleMaps: true or false
- acceptedAt: the date and time of the decision
- version: the consent-text version

This record is technically necessary for remembering the visitor's privacy choice. It is not used for analytics, advertising, or profiling.

## Optional service: Google Maps

Google Maps is enabled by default in the settings screen but does not load until the visitor accepts or saves that choice. Rejecting optional services keeps all Google Maps frames blocked.

Visitors can review or change this preference at any time with the cookie icon in the bottom-right corner of the website.

## Services not used by this boilerplate

No analytics, marketing pixels, advertising cookies, newsletter tracking, or user-profiling services are configured.
